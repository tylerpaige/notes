#### Digital reporting requires pockets full of affordable kit. Here’s some empowering technology – but still pack a notepad

#### GoPro

The GoPro has established itself as the final word in HD video for sports. However, it has equally useful applications for journalists who need to work hands-free or in challenging environments, whether at protests (it has a hard, waterproof shell), in extreme weather or, when the need arises, to film covertly.

#### Canon S120

For more image control and the ability to switch from video to stills, an HD point-and-shoot like the [Canon S120](http://www.canon.co.uk/For_Home/Product_Finder/Cameras/Digital_Camera/PowerShot/PowerShot_S120/) is a genuinely handy piece of kit. It's more practical and less conspicuous than a DSLR, so forget about looking flash. If your reporting skills are up to snuff, this pocket camera could be all you need to tell a great story.
