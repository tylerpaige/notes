## Highlights
- The rise of front-facing cameras and platforms like TikTok has turned everyday life into a continuous site of production, where identity is not simply expressed but actively worked on in real time. Ordinary activities like getting dressed, making breakfast, going to the gym, ordering coffee, are performed with an audience in mind (real or imagined).
