With Modernism increasingly becoming an aesthetic or nostalgic design choice instead of a philosophical one, these personalities who rallied against modernist “objectivity” and proposed a more personal route of design began to loom high over the profession’s discourse. The argument of making design more personal morphed into a narcissistic view of our profession where the personality came first, and the work came second.
